Tomotoa's legend

It is known in the ancient woods of asia to be careful where you tread. As next to humans and animal other creatures are rumored to linger in the tops of the trees. Tomotoa's, the creatures of the woods are said to always look down from the trees and keep an eye on everything that goes on in the woods. The legend of Tomotoa is often used to make children behave, if they don't, Tomotoa's might just come to take them into the woods. Legend has it Tomotoa live off the souls of nature abusers such as people who litter, cut trees or start fires. Hence, it took non-indiginous people to start cutting trees for the wood industry. Loacls were too suporstitious to ever do such a thing. The legend gained fame when a tree fell on top of a woodchopper during their first week, the cause never discovered.
